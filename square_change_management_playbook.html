Account setup & pre-configuration — days 1–7

Your dedicated onboarding specialist coordinates everything. You provide information; we do the heavy lifting.

Location structure confirmed (single, multi-location, or franchise)
Banking and deposit settings connected
Pricing confirmed and locked

Menu, inventory & hardware — days 7–21

We build your catalog, configure your hardware, and test everything before go-live.

Menu/inventory items imported or built

Staff training & go-live — days 21–45

Your team gets hands-on training before your first real transaction.

Kickoff call with your full team

Optimization & growth — days 45–180

Once live, we focus on unlocking more value — loyalty, advanced reporting, revenue tools.

Loyalty program activated
Revenue reports reviewed with your team
Expansion to additional locations (if applicable)
